College is one of the most important decisions of of a person's life. The first time on campus can be a bit overwhelming to proscective students, parents and even professors. How do you make it to the right classroom? What clubs and activities are offered? And most importantly, Where is can you get coffee? Campus Life is an interactive campus tour that can not only gives directions to any location on campus and spit out historical and insteresting facts about sites, but can assist in making the decesion to accept admission.

Technology: Mapping Applications, GPS, virtual tour, database, social interaction with Facebook/Google+/Twitter, and Gamification.

Market: Prospective Students, Parents and Professors.

Phase 1: Start with one college. Take campus map and integrate into an interactive tour. Allow Mobile phone to give GPS location and map to desired campus location.
